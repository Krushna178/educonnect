Books are an essential part of NEET preparation, providing in-depth coverage of the syllabus and practice questions to help students excel in the exam. Here's some information about books for NEET preparation:

1)NCERT Textbooks:

NCERT books are the foundation for NEET preparation. They provide comprehensive coverage of the syllabus and are highly recommended by experts. Students should thoroughly study NCERT textbooks for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and other relevant subjects.

2)Reference Books:

In addition to NCERT books, students often use reference books authored by subject matter experts to supplement their preparation. Some popular reference books for NEET preparation include:
a)Physics: Concepts of Physics by HC Verma, Fundamentals of Physics by Halliday, Resnick & Walker, Objective Physics by DC Pandey.
b)Chemistry: Physical Chemistry by OP Tandon, Organic Chemistry by Morrison & Boyd, Concise Inorganic Chemistry by JD Lee.
c)Biology: Objective Biology by Dinesh, Trueman's Biology series, Biology Vol. 1 & 2 by GRB Bathla Publications.

3)Practice Books:

Practice books contain a collection of practice questions, solved examples, and previous year questions for NEET preparation. They help students test their understanding of concepts and practice solving problems. Some popular practice books include: a)NEET/AIPMT Physics: 29 Years' Chapterwise Solutions by MTG Editorial Board.
b)NEET/AIPMT Chemistry: 40 Days Chemistry for NEET by Sudhanshu Thakur.
c)NEET/AIPMT Biology: 40 Days Biology for NEET by S. Chakravarty.

4)Mock Test Books:

Mock test books simulate the NEET exam environment and help students assess their preparation level. They contain full-length mock tests, model question papers, and previous year question papers. Some popular mock test books include: 28 Years' CBSE-NEET & AIPMT Solved Papers by Arihant Experts. a)NEET/AIPMT Challenger Physics by MTG Editorial Board.
b)NEET/AIPMT Challenger Chemistry by MTG Editorial Board.
c)NEET/AIPMT Challenger Biology by MTG Editorial Board.

5)Online Resources:

In addition to printed books, there are various online resources available for NEET preparation, including e-books, video lectures, quizzes, and study materials. Platforms like Embibe, Khan Academy, and Topper offer comprehensive study resources for NEET preparation.

When selecting books for NEET preparation, it's essential to choose reliable and up-to-date sources that cover the entire syllabus comprehensively. Additionally, students should create a structured study plan and utilize a combination of books, practice materials, and online resources to maximize their preparation efforts.
