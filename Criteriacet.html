The MHT CET (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test) is an entrance exam conducted by the Government of Maharashtra for admission to various undergraduate courses in engineering, pharmacy, and agriculture in colleges across the state. Here's a detailed overview of the eligibility criteria for MHT CET:

Nationality:

Indian Nationality is mandatory for appearing in the MHT CET.

Domicile:

Candidates should be a domicile of Maharashtra. However, there are some seats available for candidates from outside Maharashtra as well.

Educational Qualifications:

For Engineering and Pharmacy Courses: Candidates must have passed 10+2 or its equivalent examination from a recognized board. They should have studied Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational subject.

Minimum aggregate marks required:

General category: 50% marks in PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) combined. Reserved category (SC/ST/OBC): 45% marks in PCM. Candidates appearing for the qualifying examination are also eligible to apply.

For Agriculture Courses:

Candidates must have passed 10+2 or its equivalent examination from a recognized board. They should have studied Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English as compulsory subjects.

Minimum aggregate marks required:

General category: 50% marks in PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) combined. Reserved category (SC/ST/OBC): 45% marks in PCB. Candidates appearing for the qualifying examination are also eligible to apply.

Age Limit:

There is no specific age limit for candidates appearing in MHT CET. However, candidates must meet the educational qualifications mentioned above.

Additional Requirements:

Candidates must have a valid Aadhar Card. They need to have a valid email ID and mobile number for communication purposes.

Reservation:

Reservation policies are applicable as per the rules and regulations of the Government of Maharashtra. Reservation is provided for candidates belonging to SC, ST, VJ/DT- NT(A), NT(B), NT(C), NT(D), OBC, SBC, EWS, and other categories as per the norms.

Important Points:

The eligibility criteria may vary slightly depending on the course and college. Candidates are advised to check the official website of MHT CET for the latest updates and notifications regarding eligibility criteria and other details. It's essential for candidates to fulfill all the eligibility criteria before applying for the exam to avoid disqualification at later stages. By meeting the eligibility criteria and preparing well for the MHT CET exam, candidates can enhance their chances of securing admission to their desired courses in colleges across Maharashtra.
