Here is some information about JEE study materials:

1)BYJU'S:

BYJU'S offers comprehensive and updated study material for IIT JEE, covering subjects like physics, chemistry, and maths. They provide downloadable PDFs, formulas, chapter-wise notes, past year papers, and more. You can access their study material at BYJU'S JEE Study Material[1].

2)Vedantu:

Vedantu provides free online study material for JEE Main and Advanced preparation. You can download PDFs of notes, question papers, and mock tests to enhance your preparation. They also offer live classes and courses taught by experts. You can find the study material at Vedantu JEE Study Material

3)Embibe:

Embibe offers study material for JEE Main 2024 exam, including notes, PDFs, question papers, and reference books for subjects like physics, chemistry, and maths. They provide mock tests and previous year solutions as well. You can access their study material at Embibe JEE Main Study Material

4)Unacademy:

Unacademy provides comprehensive and well-structured JEE study materials designed by top IITians. Their study material includes syllabus, previous year papers, important formulas, and more for JEE Main and Advanced. You can find their study material at Unacademy JEE Study Material

5)askIITians:

askIITians offers free study material for IIT JEE, NEET (AIPMT), and other engineering and medical exams. Their study material covers all topics and features solved examples, diagrams, and tables for better understanding. You can find their study material at askIITians IIT JEE Study Material
